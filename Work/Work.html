How Volunteer Overseas Works
1
Find your perfect project
Research and compare the best projects abroad based on what matters to you:
LocationWhere's your dream destination?
ActivityWhat do you see yourself doing?
DurationHow much time do you have?
Start datesFix and flexible start dates(varies by program)?
2
Apply Now
Follow our easy and secure application process:
1
Fill out your trip preferences
2
Tell your program why you are good fit
3
Reserve your spot with small deposit*
*This deposit is generally a small portion of the total project fee. Your deposit secure your spot with the program and shows that you are a serious applicant. this deposit is fully refundable if you are not accepted by the program.
3
Wait to hear Great News
After you submit your application, you will hear from your program within 3-7 business days.
Applying through Volunteer Overseas automatically qualifies you to:
Enter to win a scholarship from Volunteer forever (up to 1,000)
Raise funds for your trip on Volunteer forever's no-fee crowdfunding platform
Talk about great news!
4
Finalize your trip
After you've been accepted,your program will help you with the not so little details:
The remainder of your program fee
final travel arrengements
your itinerary and work schedule
Your medical, dietary or other personal needs
